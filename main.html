Get To Know

How it Works

Set Timer

Enter the time in minutes on how long you want the extension running. Start the timer and the countdown will follow.

Be Productive!

It is time for you to do whatever you supposed to do; those assignments that you've been procrastinating.

Captured

When the timer ends, a bunch of randomized screenshots will be downloaded in your local device.

Get the most of our extension

Suggestion

Change Your Browser Setting

The browser might show that the picture have been downloaded directly to you. It can be disturbing. Dont worry you can just turn it off from your browser setting by navigating to the download setting.

Share it with your friends!

Invite your friends to use our extension. You can share the screenshots you got with your friends and see who has been slacking off.

Reflect!

Take a look of what you have done over the time period! If you see some productive things, great job! If you see yourself getting distracted most of the times, it is time to reflect!

Keep an Eye!

Watch over those who matters! It could be your kids, students, or subordinates. Make sure they are on the right track.
